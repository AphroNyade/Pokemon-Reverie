Welcome to the Pokémon Reverie Wiki

Pokémon Reverie is a fan-made Pokémon game. This game includes features such as new custom mega and regional evolutions, an entire region and a thick plot.

The goal of this wiki is to provide a thorough guide of the game, with every information you might need, as well as detailed listing of each route and town's interactives.
